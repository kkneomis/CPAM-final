Cyber Pathways Across Maryland (CPAM)

On October 1, 2014, the U. S. Department of Labor awarded a $14.9 million Trade Adjustment Assistance Community College and Career Training grant to Montgomery College and 13 other community colleges within the State of Maryland for a performance period of 3 years. This project will address the challenges of an lack of adequate pool of skilled cyber workers, the shortage of laboratory facilities to support cyber training, a lack of training in flexible modalities, and barriers to learning for underserved populations.

Anne Arundel Community College

AACC's nationally recognized Cybersecurity degree programs and certificates provide knowledge of information assurance, cybersecurity, digital forensics and network technology. Courses include practical experience with current tools and techniques. Graduates of these programs can enter the workforce or continue their education at a four-year institution. AACC has been designated a National Center of Academic Excellence 2Y by the National Security Agency and U.S. Department of Homeland Security.

Prince George’s Community College

Prince George’s Community College (PGCC,) the home of National Cyber Watch, recognized for Cybersecurity degree programs and certificates provides education in the area of information security, cybersecurity, cybersecurity management and the newly designed “ Career Advantage: Security Clearance and Background Checks”. The courses, which lead to various certificates, are developed to entail state of the art technology and techniques with practical and hands on experienced by certified professionals. Graduates of these programs can enter the workforce or continue their education at a four-year institution. PGCC is designated a National Center of Academic Excellence 2Y by the National Security Agency and U.S. Department of Homeland Security.

  • Cybersecurity Assoc. of applied Science
  • Cybersecurity Certificate
  • Cyber & Tech Training
  • Cybersecurity Management Certificate
  • Career Advantages: Introduction to Security Clearance and background checks
